Restaurant

Project Overview

My website simplifies the discovery of deals and information for a local restaurant. Utilizing JavaScript libraries, HTML, CSS, and jQuery, I've designed an intuitive interface for users to explore menus, place orders, and access essential restaurant details.

PROBLEM

Despite the simplicity of finding local restaurant deals, challenges such as filtering and sorting items persist. My goal is to overcome these obstacles by creating a user-friendly platform that enhances the dining experience through innovative design and technology.
