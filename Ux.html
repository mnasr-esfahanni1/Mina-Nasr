Nature Cycle

Project Overview

Introducing the NaturCycle app: Your gateway to sustainable beauty. Discover eco-friendly skincare, educational resources, and join our recycling program. Embrace innovation and environmental responsibility with every tap.

PROBLEM

NaturCycle: Simplifying sustainable skincare by offering eco-friendly options and promoting the benefits of recycling human waste in cosmetics.

PROJECT VISION

The NaturCycle project vision is to transform the beauty industry with an intuitive mobile app promoting sustainable practices. By connecting eco-conscious users to eco-friendly skincare derived from recycled human waste, NaturCycle empowers informed decisions and active participation in waste reduction, fostering a greener beauty approach.

Journey Mapping & User Flow

In this UX/UI project, I prioritized creating a seamless journey map and user flow. Through analysis and research, I crafted intuitive touchpoints to enhance user navigation. Iterative refinement based on journey map insights aimed to optimize usability and satisfaction.

From Sketches to Wireframes

In the initial stages of design,I started with quick sketches to explore ideas. These evolved into wireframes, simplifying the interface layout. These wireframes were essential for communicating design concepts effectively.

From Wireframes to Interactive Mockups

For the prototype, I translated wireframes into interactive digital mockups using design tools. This allowed for hands-on testing and refinement of the user experience before final implementation.
